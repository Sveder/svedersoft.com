A unique and stylish board game

We all know and love the classic board game "four in a row". It's simple to understand but hard to master. It's a challenging battle of the minds between two players, each racing to complete a row, a column or a diagonal with four of his coins. This rendition of the game features great artwork and a stylish, clean wood design that is pretty but without distracting from the game itself. It also has original sounds that emphasize the thrill of dropping that last coin, dooming your opponent to shame, at least until the next game.


So, why are you still reading this? Get the game and play - four the win!
